About the Developer

Farhan Sayyed is the developer behind this automated trading system, bringing over 7 years of experience in the financial markets. Throughout this journey, the focus has been on understanding market behavior, refining trading strategies, and developing a disciplined approach to execution.

With time, it became clear that emotional decision-making is one of the biggest challenges traders face. This led to the development of a rule-based system designed to remove guesswork and follow predefined trading logic with consistency.

The system is built around structured strategies, fixed risk parameters, and controlled execution, aiming to create a more systematic way of participating in the markets. Every component is designed with simplicity, clarity, and discipline in mind, making it suitable for traders who prefer a rules-driven approach.

This project reflects a commitment to continuous improvement, practical strategy design, and a focus on long-term consistency rather than short-term hype.
